Frequently Asked Questions

Our taro chips are made from locally sourced, organic taro roots. We slice them extra thin and cook them in small batches using premium oils. This results in a lighter, crispier chip with the perfect crunch. We also use unique spice blends created by our culinary team to offer flavors you won't find anywhere else.

Yes! All of our taro chips are 100% gluten-free and produced in a dedicated gluten-free facility to prevent any cross-contamination. They're perfect for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

Our special packaging keeps the chips fresh for up to 6 months from the production date, which is clearly marked on each bag. Once opened, we recommend consuming them within 5-7 days for maximum freshness and crunch.

Absolutely! We're committed to sustainability. Our bags are made from plant-based materials that are biodegradable and compostable. The outer shipping boxes are made from 100% recycled cardboard, and we use paper-based tape. We're constantly looking for ways to reduce our environmental footprint.
